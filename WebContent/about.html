About VIT ALIGARH

VIT-ALIGARH

Vision Institute of Technology-Aligarh (VIT-A) is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. A .P.J.Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (AKTU Code 524). VIT-Aligarh is also affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Lucknow for Polytechnic(Diploma) Programs (BTE Code 1629) VIT-Aligarh is also affiliated to NCVT for ITI courses (Code PU099900834)

Certification Courses & Programs

Students of VIT get an excellent opportunity to pursue Certification Courses while pursuing the regular programs. These Certification Courses are tailor made as per the industry requirements and have few cutting edge market technologies. The Courses consist to Theory, Labs, Online Study Materials & Guest Lectures/Workshops.
